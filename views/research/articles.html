Faculty Research Contributions (2025)

The Department of Networking and Communications proudly presents its faculty members’ research contributions published in reputed international journals during July to September 2025. The publications span multiple domains including Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, IoT, Healthcare, and Environmental Sustainability, reflecting the department’s multidisciplinary research excellence.

Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning
FEWGAT-AGCE: A Novel Deep Learning Approach for Early Alzheimer’s Disease Detection Using Hippocampal Subfields and Cognitive Assessments

Authors: M. Thamizharasi, M. Lakshmi

Journal: International Journal of Machine Learning and Cybernetics

ISSN: 1868-8071 | Year: 2025

Introduces a novel deep learning framework combining hippocampal imaging and cognitive scores for early detection of Alzheimer’s disease.

Optimizing Alzheimer’s Disease Detection with Dual Attention CNN and Gooseneck Barnacle Search

Authors: M. Thamizharasi, M. Lakshmi

Journal: Cybernetics and Systems

ISSN: 0196-9722 | Year: 2025

Presents a dual-attention CNN enhanced with a novel optimization algorithm to improve Alzheimer’s disease classification accuracy.

Propagation Routing for Hybrid Bandwidth Allocation with Reinforcement Learning in VANET Infrastructure Analysis

Authors: G. Michaela, A. Selvakumar, S. Manikandan, R. Gnanjeyaraman, U. Arul

Journal: Automatika

ISSN: 0005-1144 | Year: 2025

Utilizes reinforcement learning for dynamic routing and efficient bandwidth allocation in vehicular ad hoc networks.

Innovative Deep Learning Models for Accurate Segmentation and Classification in Oncological Diagnosis Data

Authors: Archana R, Anand L

Journal: Journal of Machine and Computing

ISSN: 2788-7669 | Year: 2025

Proposes advanced deep learning models to enhance segmentation and classification in cancer diagnosis datasets.

Cybersecurity and Cryptography
Analysis of Various Homomorphic Encryption Algorithms Based on Primitive Functions and Applications

Authors: Josepha Menandas, Mary Subaja Christo

Journal: OPSEARCH

ISSN: 0975-0320 | Year: 2025

Reviews and evaluates homomorphic encryption algorithms for secure computation and privacy-preserving applications.

An Efficient Integrity and Authenticated Elliptic Curve Cryptography Algorithm for Secure Storage and Routing in TLS/SSL

Authors: Josepha Menandas, Mary Subaja Christo

Journal: International Arab Journal of Information Technology

ISSN: 2309-4524 | Year: 2025

Proposes an ECC-based cryptographic scheme improving data integrity and secure transmission in TLS/SSL frameworks.

A Comprehensive Integrated Security Model with Advanced Access Control for Enhancing WSN Computing in IoT Frameworks

Authors: Navaneethan S, Arun A

Journal: Technical Gazette

ISSN: 1330-3651 | Year: 2025

Develops a layered security model to enhance wireless sensor network protection within IoT ecosystems.

Deep Stacked Kronecker Network for Intrusion Detection System in IoT Environment with Big Data

Authors: J. Sreemathy, Arun A., S. Sureshu, S. Mahaboob Basha

Journal: Cybernetics and Systems

ISSN: 1087-6553 | Year: 2025

Introduces a deep stacked Kronecker neural network for intrusion detection in IoT-based big data environments.

IoT and Smart Systems
Energy-Efficient Blockchain-Integrated IoT and AI Framework for Sustainable Urban Microclimate Management

Authors: N. Krishnaraj, Hadeel Alsolai, Fahd N. Al-Wesabi, Yahia Said, Ali Alqazzaz, S. Gayathri Priya, S. Shanmathi, B. Narmada

Smart Health System with Deep Kronecker Network-Based Key Generation for Privacy-Aware Aggregation Authentication and Access Control in IoT

ISSN: 1573-2886 | Year: 2025

Scalable and Transparent Mental Health Support via XAI-LLM
