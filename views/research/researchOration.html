RESEARCH ORATION

The Department of Networking and Communications organized a series of Research Oration sessions from July to October 2025, providing a vibrant platform for full-time Ph.D. scholars to present their ongoing research. These sessions aimed to enhance academic discourse, promote innovative thinking, and encourage interdisciplinary collaboration.

Mrs. D. Suganya (Reg. No: RA2415003011005)

Supervisor: Dr. Arun A

Title: Exploring Neural Network Types for Image Processing

Date: 26.07.2025

The presentation explored CNNs, RNNs, and GANs, highlighting advancements in pattern recognition, image classification, and object detection.

Ms. Krithiga M (Reg. No: RA2415003011017)

Supervisor: Dr. Radhika R

Title: Deep Learning in Medical Image Processing

Date: 09.08.2025

Focused on disease detection, segmentation, and challenges such as data availability and model interpretability.

Mrs. Bhuvaneswari Perumal (Reg. No: RA2415003011004)

Supervisor: Dr. Vaishnavi Moorthy

Title: Deep Generative Model

Date: 23.08.2025

Introduced VAEs and GANs for data augmentation, anomaly detection, and image enhancement.

Mrs. Vidhubala J (Reg. No: RC2513003011006)

Supervisor: Dr. R. Kayalvizhi

Title: Introduction to IoT Security

Date: 13.09.2025

Addressed IoT security challenges including privacy, authentication, and intrusion prevention.

Mr. Jayaraman (Reg. No: RC2513003011011)

Supervisor: Dr. Naresh

Title: Introduction to Quantum Computing

Date: 27.09.2025

Explained qubits, superposition, and entanglement with applications in cryptography and secure communication.

Event Highlights

The sessions showcased the department’s dedication to nurturing research excellence and innovation through active scholar–faculty interaction.

“Research is not just about finding answers — it’s about redefining the questions.”

“Knowledge shared is innovation multiplied.”
