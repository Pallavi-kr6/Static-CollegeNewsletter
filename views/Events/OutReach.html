Outreach Activities

Farm Connect 2025

Date: 29.07.2025
Time: 10:00 AM – 2:30 PM
Venue: Farm Science Centre (ICAR - KVK), Kattupakkam
Participants: 30 Members
Association: Krishi Vignan Kendra, Tamil Nadu
Conveners & Co-Conveners: Dr. Nallarasan V,
Dr. Praveena Akki
Resource Person: Dr. Aparna,
Associate Scientist,
Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University

Event Summary

Farm Connect 2025 was a knowledge-filled journey that offered deep insights into agriculture, veterinary sciences, and the transformative role of modern technology in farming. The event highlighted integrated farming systems, sustainable agriculture practices, animal nutrition, veterinary care, and real-time demonstrations in crop management.

Sessions also showcased the use of IoT, sensors, and data-driven precision farming, along with the significant role of Computer Science in AgriTech and rural innovation. The program, facilitated by the conveners, faculty, NWC Association students, and the Farm Science Centre team, served as a perfect example of how engineering minds can contribute to sustainability, food security, and rural transformation.

The event concluded with a valedictory session and vote of thanks by the coordinators.

Light Up Knowledge

Date: 14.08.2025
Time: 10:00 AM – 1:00 PM
Venue: Panchayat Union Middle School, Ninnaikarai, Maraimalainagar
Participants: 85 students
Conveners: Dr. T.R. Vedhavathy,
Dr. M. Mahalakshmi,
Dr. B. Lakshmi Dhevi

Event Summary

The outreach activity successfully engaged Class VII and VIII students in exploring the concepts and properties of light energy, including reflection, refraction, and dispersion, through real-time demonstrations, interactive experiments, and group discussions. Students actively participated by observing, questioning, predicting, and testing outcomes, which enhanced their understanding and bridged the gap between theory and practice.

The session nurtured curiosity, critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills. Student volunteers conducted a quiz to reinforce learning, and prizes were awarded to the top performers, adding motivation and excitement. Distribution of stationary and snacks created a positive, encouraging environment. Overall, the activity deepened students’ comprehension of scientific concepts, inspired active learning, and fostered a lifelong interest and enthusiasm for science.

Medical Camp: Success Story!!

Date: 20.09.2025
Time: 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM
Venue: Shiri Sarada Sakthi Peedam Ashram, Old Perungalathur
Participants: 205 Members
Association: National Service Scheme and Department of Community Medicine, SRMIST
Conveners: Dr. Yamini B,
Dr. S. Muruganantham
Co-Conveners: Dr. Metilda Florence S,
Dr. Joseph Raymond V,
Dr. Fancy C,
Dr. Anand M

Event Summary

The Department of Networking and Communications (NWC), in collaboration with the National Service Scheme (NSS) and the Department of Community Medicine, SRMIST, organized a Medical Camp as part of the National Health Mission’s campaign, “Healthy Women, Strong Family (Swasth Nari, Sashakt Parivar Abhiyaan).” The initiative reflected SRM’s spirit of unity, compassion, and service.

With the dedicated efforts of six NWC faculty members, twenty-two doctors from Community Medicine, NSS faculty coordinators, and seven enthusiastic student volunteers, the camp created a meaningful social impact. The outreach program benefited 105 students and 100 women, offering health awareness, consultation, and care. Special appreciation to Ms. Bharathi Devi T, Mr. Raja P, and Mr. Selva Kumar for their valuable support.
