Emerging Technologies

Date: 25.07.2025
Time: 10:30 AM
Venue: MS-Teams
No. of Participants: 45
Resource Person:
Mr. Jayant Pathak
(B.Tech IT-2020–2024),
Information Security Analyst
Convener:
Dr. Venkatesh K,
Dr. Nimala K,
Dr. Godwin Ponsam J,
Dr. V. Joseph Raymond,
Dr. D. Saveetha,
Dr. N. Prasath

Event Summary

The event explored how powerful technologies will impact industries, creating new job opportunities, solving real-world problems, driving sustainability, and enabling smart living.

Event Outcome

The session provided valuable insights to participants from II, III, and IV year on how next-generation technologies are transforming industries and society. The event emphasized the role of AI, IoT, Blockchain, 5G, VR/AR, and Quantum Computing in solving real-world problems, driving sustainability, and enabling smart living. Students gained a clear understanding of why these technologies are crucial in the modern world and how they are shaping new job opportunities and innovation landscapes.

The speaker encouraged participants to explore, research, and develop creative ideas using these technologies, fostering curiosity and forward-thinking. Overall, the outcome was an enhanced awareness of emerging technologies and their practical applications, inspiring students to prepare for future industry needs.

Importance of Cyber Security

Date: 22.08.2025
Time: 2:30 to 4:00 PM
Venue: TP 404/405
No. of Participants: 65
Resource Person:
Mr. Akash Periyasamy,
Cyber Forensic Analyst,
Kriyavan Cyber Forensics, India
Convener:
Dr. Venkatesh K,
Dr. Nimala K,
Dr. Godwin Ponsam J,
Dr. V. Joseph Raymond,
Dr. D. Saveetha,
Dr. N. Prasath

Event Outcome

The session enabled participants from II and III Year to gain a comprehensive understanding of why cybersecurity is critical in today’s digital era. The event highlighted how effective security safeguards personal and professional data from hackers, thereby preventing financial loss, identity theft, and data misuse.

Students learned how cybersecurity fosters trust in online banking, e-commerce, and digital communication, while ensuring safe interactions in an increasingly connected world. The speaker shared practical use cases from digital forensics and introduced tools for protecting computers and information systems. The outcome was a deeper awareness that cybersecurity is not just a technical defense but a shared responsibility vital for building a secure digital society.
