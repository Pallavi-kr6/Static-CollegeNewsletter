Guest Lecture: XAI – Scope, Impact & Research Opportunities

Date: 02.08.2025

Time: 09:30 AM – 12:30 PM

Venue: Dr. Ramanujan Hall, Tech Park – I (Lobby)

Convener:
Dr. M. Saravanan (Professor, NWC),
Dr. P. Balamurugan (Associate Professor, NWC),
Mrs. V. Vijayalakshmi (Assistant Professor, NWC)

Resource Person:
Dr. Seshathiri Dhanasekaran, CTO & AI Research Leader, UiT The Arctic University of Norway

Number of Registered Participants: 25

The guest lecture successfully provided participants with a comprehensive understanding of the scope, impact, and research opportunities in Explainable AI (XAI). Scholars and faculty gained insights into how XAI can enhance transparency, interpretability, and accountability in AI systems, addressing challenges in decision-making and ethical considerations.

The session inspired attendees to explore innovative research directions, interdisciplinary collaborations, and practical applications of XAI in real-world scenarios. By highlighting emerging trends and open research questions, the event motivated participants to engage in critical thinking, experimentation, and knowledge-sharing.

Overall, the outcome was heightened awareness of the importance of explainable AI, strengthened research curiosity, and encouragement to pursue impactful projects, fostering both academic growth and global scientific collaboration.
