Dr. Annapurani Panaiyappan K, Dr. C. Malathy, Dr. G. Abinaya, and Dr. G. Ramya won First Place in the Hackathon conducted by C-DAC, Bengaluru on 4th August 2025. The team’s innovative project, “Smart Parking System,” earned them the top honor during the five-day Faculty Development Program titled “Idea to Prototype to Product”, organized by C-DAC, Hyderabad..
